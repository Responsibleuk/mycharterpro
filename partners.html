PARTNERS & AFFILIATES

M/Y Charter Pro operates a number of selected partner programmes with hotels, tour operators, media partners and agencies. These are optional programmes and allow owners and managers to automatically make their yachts available to these additional markets and provide partners the opportunity to build direct relationships with aspirational products and share these benefits exclusuively with their clientelle.

Broker Programme

M/Y Charter Pro offers real-time access to yacht chartering for consumers and brokers, with personalised booking experiences aligned to the broker's brand. The program provides brokers with unparalleled access to high-quality charter yachts and the opportunity to drive sales digitally. Reservations can be made online or through the brokerage team's client portal, with affiliation partner payments triggered based on the booking value.

Hotel Programme

M/Y Charter Pro connects hotel clients with high-value experiences to increase the value of their stay and earnings for partner hotels.
