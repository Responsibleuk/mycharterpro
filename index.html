How It Works

Begin your blue water adventure by selecting the location you want and the yacht available at you port of choice. Once you find the vessel and dates you want, simply click book. You will be provided with the booking terms and conditions, so everything is entirely transparent, keeping you in complete control from the start. Only if the yacht is available, you will be asked to confirm the reservation with a deposit (normally 10% of the booking value), which you can do by card so that you are fully protected.

Once you have secured the yacht, the yacht manager will make contact to finalise the details of your charter and confirm the final balance due, all using the M/Y Charter Pro system, so that all your details payments and booking arrangements are managed securely.

Destinations

Malta and its surrounding islands offers yacht charters a fantastic combination of UNESCO world heritage ports; unspoilt anchorages; beautiful historical architecture from Neolithic temples and greek fables through to renowned Knights Templar as well as a vibrant night life in its cities.

Its long and consistent season, running from April to October, make it perfect for those wanting to enjoy everything synonymous with the best of mediterranean crusing. Its cyrstal blue waters, unspoilt sunkissed beaches, hidden coves and diving areas make it ideal for making full use of all the amenities on your charter yacht, whether a full super yacht, sports yacht or sailing yacht, you won’t be disappointed.

Itinieraries in Malta offer the opportunity to explore the main island, as well as the sister Isles of Gozo, famed home to Calypso in Homers Odyssey and beautiful car free unspoilt Camino, renowned for snorkling and diving with its own blue lagoon.

Your Questions Answered

M/Y Charter Pro allows you to fully manage your booking throughout the pre-departure period, within the terms and conditions set by the yacht manager. The terms and conditions applicable are available to you to view at any time and normally include a one week from booking no quibble cancellation period unless the departure date is within two weeks of departure.

In some regions it may be possible to charter a yacht without a license, however, regardless of whether you are an experienced sailor or not, chartering a yacht with a skipper means you not only have an expert sailor on hand, but also one who knows all the best spots, will plan everything for you, enabling you and your guests to simply relax an enjoy the experience.

The yacht listing will normally identify costs that are included or in addition to the charter rate. Typically additional costs will include food and entertainment; transit, or marina and anchorage fees, as well as associated taxes; and fuel. For crewed charters the normal industry expectation is to tip between 5% - 15% of the weekly charter cost (excluding running costs, taxes and fees).
