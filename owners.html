Yacht Owners

Introduction

M/Y Charter Pro providers yacht owners and managers a unique way to manage their yacht portfolio, present availability direct to agents and clients and manage their charter process on line twenty four hours a day, seven days a week, no matter what time zone they, their clients, or agents may be based.

M/Y Charter Pro is packed with great features that maximise sales opportunities and reduce yacht and broker administration. It can integrate with websites and social media feeds, allowing clients and agents to instantly reserve, pay deposits (and final balances), providing them with the confidence that the yacht of their choice is secured for their specified dates and the yacht owner immediate secured bookings, ensuring potential clients are not lost through the traditional brokerage process.

Features To Maximise Profit

M/Y Charter Pro’s lack of reliance on the traditional online shopping basket means uniquely your clients can manage, amend and reschedule their own bookings online, within the terms you set, so you don’t have to, delivering happy customers and no administration.

The M/Y Charter Pro planner manages all of your online and direct (walk-in) bookings, so your time can be focussed on delivering exceptional service. The M/Y Charter pro diary will allow you to block off times for personal use, maintenance and turnarounds, so that you can manage everything in one place, whether chartering or not.

The Wallet ensures your transactions are recorded for each charter and always updated on the live system, from initial deposits to balance (pre-departure) payments, the wallet handles everything for you. The Wallet is able to receive payments through all major cards and via bank transfer, providing owners, managers and cleints with security throughout.

Drive your own online bookings with M/Y Charter Pro by integrating with your own website and social media platforms. This maintains all the functionality of the M/Y Charter Pro marketplace platform, but at a fraction of the cost and time required to administer these bookings manually – and we’re open to take payments 24/7!
